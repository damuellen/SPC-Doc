Enumeration Design 

public enum Design

The enum contains static properties that indicate whether the plant's design includes specific components. These properties are based on the Layout instance created in the enum. The properties represent the presence or absence of a solar field, heater, boiler, gas turbine, and thermal storage in the plant's design.
