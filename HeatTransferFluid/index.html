Structure Heat​Transfer​Fluid 

public struct HeatTransferFluid: CustomStringConvertible, Equatable

The Heat Transfer Fluid is characterized through maximum operating temperature, freeze temperature, specific heat capacity, viscosity, thermal conductivity, enthalpy, and density as a function of temperature.
